# TAURON 2026 Tariff Components — Group G

Sources:
- **TS** — TAURON Sprzedaż sp. z o.o. tariff (electricity, valid 1 Jan 2026 – 31 Dec 2026)
- **TD** — TAURON Dystrybucja S.A. distribution tariff 2026

> All prices are **gross (brutto)**, i.e. include excise tax (0.005 zł/kWh) and VAT (23%).
